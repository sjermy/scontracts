Basic commands in geth:

Accounts on the node: 

> eth.accounts

Create new account: 

> personal.newAccount();

Get coinbase - it has to be set for mining

> eth.coinbase 

Set coinbase:

> miner.setEtherBase(eth.accounts[0]);

Start mining:

> miner.start(1);

Get balance - in Wei: 

> eth.getBalance(eth.accounts[0]);

Or in ether:

> web3.fromWei(eth.getBalance(eth.account[0]), “ether”);

Stop mining:

> miner.stop();
